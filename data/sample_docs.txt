2. Leave Policy
================================================================================

Document ID: XYZ-HR-LEAVE-002
Title: Employee Leave Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Applicable To: All full-time employees

Annual Leave Entitlements:
- Casual Leave: 12 days per year
- Sick Leave: 12 days per year
- Earned Leave: 18 days per year (accrues at 1.5 days/month)
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks (as per Maternity Benefit Act)
- Paternity Leave: 5 days
- Bereavement Leave: 3 days per incident

Leave Carry Forward:
- Earned Leave: Up to 30 days can be carried forward
- Casual and Sick Leave: Cannot be carried forward

Leave Application Process:
1. Login to HRMS portal at hrms.xyzenterprises.com
2. Navigate to Leave Management > Apply Leave
3. Select leave type, date range, and provide reason
4. Submit for manager approval

Approval SLA:
- Casual / Earned Leave: Manager must approve within 2 business days
- Medical Leave (>3 days): Requires HR approval and medical certificate

Leave Encashment:
- Earned leave beyond 30 days is eligible for encashment at year-end
- Encashment value = (Basic Salary / 26) x Number of leave days encashed

Leave Without Pay (LWP):
- Employees who exhaust all leave balances may apply for LWP
- LWP requires VP-level approval
- LWP impacts monthly payroll deductions

5. IT Issue Logs
================================================================================

Document ID: XYZ-IT-ISSUE-005
Title: Common IT Issues and Resolutions

Issue #1: VPN Connection Failure
Symptoms: Unable to connect to corporate VPN; error "Authentication Failed"
Resolution:
1. Verify your MFA app is synced (check time settings)
2. Re-enter credentials; ensure Caps Lock is off
3. If issue persists, reset VPN credentials via IT Portal > Reset VPN Password
Escalation: If unresolved after 30 minutes, contact ithelp@xyzenterprises.com

Issue #2: HRMS Login Error
Symptoms: "Account Locked" message after multiple failed attempts
Resolution:
1. Wait 15 minutes for auto-unlock
2. Use "Forgot Password" link on login page
3. Contact IT Help Desk with your employee ID for manual unlock

Issue #3: Slow System Performance
Symptoms: Applications taking > 10 seconds to load, high CPU usage
Resolution:
1. Restart your machine
2. Close unused applications and browser tabs
3. Run Disk Cleanup (Windows) or Optimize Storage (Mac)
4. Submit a hardware review ticket if issue recurs weekly

Issue #4: Email Delivery Failure
Symptoms: Emails not reaching external recipients
Resolution:
1. Check recipient email address for typos
2. Verify attachment size is under 25MB
3. Whitelist request: Submit to IT Portal if a domain is being blocked

7. Expense Reimbursement Policy
================================================================================

Document ID: XYZ-FIN-EXP-007
Title: Employee Expense Reimbursement Policy

Eligible Expenses:
- Business travel (flights, hotels, local transport)
- Client entertainment (with manager approval, up to INR 5,000/event)
- Conference/training registration fees
- Internet allowance (INR 1,500/month for WFH employees)

Ineligible Expenses:
- Personal meals (unless traveling)
- Traffic fines or parking violations
- Alcohol (not reimbursable under any circumstances)
- Personal subscriptions

Submission Process:
1. Collect original receipts for all expenses
2. Log into Finance Portal at finance.xyzenterprises.com
3. Navigate to Expenses > New Claim
4. Upload receipts and fill in expense details
5. Submit by the 5th of the following month

Approval and Payment:
- Claims up to INR 10,000: Auto-approved with manager notification
- Claims INR 10,001 - 50,000: Manager approval required
- Claims above INR 50,000: Finance Head approval required
- Approved claims are processed in the next payroll cycle

Travel Advance:
Employees can request a travel advance up to 80% of estimated travel cost. Advances must be settled within 7 days of return.
